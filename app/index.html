At Treeco, we have over 13 years of experience in the tree service industry.

We have an ISA Certified Arborist on staff, with additional Tree Risk Assessment Qualification (TRAQ) Certification. We are fully qualified to assess any job, give advice on solutions, and are equipped with all the tools to overcome any challenges we may face.

Sustainability

Following these eco-friendly principles, we have developed the Treeco Two Tree Guarantee program: For every tree that Treeco removes, we will plant two more at a different location!

Another green initiative that Treeco is proud to present is the use of Vegetable oil in our chainsaws! This eliminates the hundreds of litres of waste petroleum oil introduced into the environment by a tree service companies every year.

Services

The following is just a few of the services we provide.

Pruning:
As Certified Arborists we can determine what type of pruning is necessary to maintain or improve the health, appearance and safety of all trees

Removal:
We will always try to promote a healthy tree, but sometimes tree removal is necessary

Stump Grinding:
We can grind as much as 12 inches below grade, including adjoining roots

Shrub and Hedge Trimming:
Let our steady hands create precision hedging effects. Our bucket-truck aids in reaching those hard-to-get places

Arborist Reports:
We are well versed in municipal regulations, and can facilitate any arborist documentation required.

Animal Rescue:
