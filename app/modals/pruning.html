Pruning

Pruning is one of the most important tasks performed by a Tree Service provider.

Structural: Trains trees to grow in a specific manner. Generally improves the strength and longevity of a tree.
Crown Reduction: Can drastically increase the amount of sunlight into an area.
Crown Raising: Removes lower branches to improve view, provide clearance for buildings, etc.
Dead-Wood Removal: Improves general health of the tree.
Wind Firming: Selectively removing branches to increase airflow through the crown. Decreases the potential for the tree to be blown over during storms.
View Enhancement:Let us create a beautiful portrait from the nature hidden behind your trees.
